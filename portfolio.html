Day Trading Simulator

FinishedDownloadable

2025

Game Designer, Gameplay Programmer

Welcome to the high-pressure world of the fruit-tech stock market! In Day Trading Simulator, you start with a modest $150 and a dream of becoming a market tycoon. Trade shares in the industry's biggest (and riskiest) players: `Lemon Inc`, `Mango Ltd`, and `Banana Corp`.

Project Notes

Built in C++ using the raylib library, I implemented a approximation of a day trading market using differing layers of perlin noise, random noise, white noise, etc. With those layers set in place, we are able to form a close approximation of the day trading experience by adjust different parameters and implementing random events such as a volatile week, sudden rise and falls by changing the type of noise being used in the current market.

Retallica

PrototypeDownloadable

2025

Game Designer, Rhythm Gameplay Programmer, UI Programmer

Rettalica is a rhythm-puzzle game where you play as a metal-themed Pied Piper guiding your squad of rats through weird, twisty industrial mazes. Hit your commands on the beat, lead your rat crew, and figure out how to escape each level without missing a step (or a squeak).

Project Notes

Tasked with implementing differing music combos, I built 3 gameplay layers. Audio syncing, A input buffer / Combo handler, and Dynamic drum generation based on user input. Bringing these three together, we are able to make it so that the player inputs musical drum-breakdowns by timing their input combos according to the background drum layer.

Rover - I

PrototypeDownloadable

2025

Gameplay Programmer

“Rover I” is a 3D Puzzle/Parkour Game that revolves around using a combination of your parkour skills and certain types of bubbles that augment your ability to traverse the environment.

Project Notes

Apart from the basic movement layer, the player is able to handle the bubble-gun. By making the player shoot different bubble types, I implemented various event handling based on the bubbles the player touches; including the jump bubble, the speed bubble, and the carry bubble which lets you cross large gaps with the caveat being you are unable to course correct.

Let's Keep Going

FinishedDownloadable

2018 / 2023

Sole Developer

A thrilling journey through the stars! Take control of a powerful rocket and fly through the endless void of space. Except the game gets faster. Rely on your fast reactions and decision making to weave through the endless void of space obstacles!

Project Notes

This game relies on infinite level generation which was achieved by two systems, The terrain generator which relies on generating the next piece by having a set of possible pieces to prevent impossible combinations. This runs recursively giving the sense of infinite level generation. The coin layer acts much simpler but similar at the same time by choosing a random set of coins to spawn in a tile, After that, it checks if said coins are possible to obtain and deletes those that do not fit the terrain tile.
