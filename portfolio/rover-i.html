Rover - I

2025

Gameplay Programmer

“Rover I” is a 3D Puzzle/Parkour Game that revolves around using a combination of your parkour skills and certain types of bubbles that augment your ability to traverse the environment.

Developer Notes

Apart from the basic movement layer, the player is able to handle the bubble-gun. By making the player shoot different bubble types, I implemented various event handling based on the bubbles the player touches; including the jump bubble, the speed bubble, and the carry bubble which lets you cross large gaps with the caveat being you are unable to course correct.
