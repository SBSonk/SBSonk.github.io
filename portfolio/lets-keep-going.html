Let's Keep Going

2018 / 2023

Sole Developer

A thrilling journey through the stars! Take control of a powerful rocket and fly through the endless void of space. Except the game gets faster. Rely on your fast reactions and decision making to weave through the endless void of space obstacles!

Developer Notes

This game relies on infinite level generation which was achieved by two systems, The terrain generator which relies on generating the next piece by having a set of possible pieces to prevent impossible combinations. This runs recursively giving the sense of infinite level generation. The coin layer acts much simpler but similar at the same time by choosing a random set of coins to spawn in a tile, After that, it checks if said coins are possible to obtain and deletes those that do not fit the terrain tile.
